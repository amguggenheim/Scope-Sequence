Unit 1: Foundational Analysis (Evidence, Structure, & Meaning)
▲
Skill Focus
Students analyze literary and informational texts to determine themes and central ideas, examine how authors develop arguments and ideas through structure and word choice, and use textual evidence to support analysis in academic discussions.
Essential Questions ▼
Q.How do we use evidence to support text analysis?
Q.How do text elements affect readers' experiences?
Q.How do readers determine the central ideas or themes of a text?
Q.How do authors' word choices influence meaning and tone?
Q.How does discussion with others deepen our understanding of texts?
🎯 Students Will Understand ▼
Themes and central ideas develop across a text through specific details and evidence.
